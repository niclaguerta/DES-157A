we were girls together

Thirteen years ago, Ke$ha found me shortly after I entered foster care. Together, we've navigated every trial and triumph, from heartbreaks to significant milestones; we've been inseparable ever since. I am deeply thankful for her companionship, as she ensured I was never alone. Here are some of the big moments we've celebrated at each address we've called home.

This is the home where we first met. Here, we excelled in our studies and finished middle school as the class valedictorian!

We weren't fond of this home, but it's where we began learning how to drive! From this point forward, we started laying the groundwork for our future.

We lived here during our senior year of high school. After graduation, we landed our first job and traveled to Yellowstone, Mexico, and Japan!

We decided to save our money and bought our first car here! This is also where we began purchasing furniture that was truly our own.

We began our journey at community college and graduated with an Associate's Degree in Business, earning honors along the way!

This is where we were accepted into UC Davis, and it's where we are now. We'll be moving again next week, but it will be okay since we have each other.
